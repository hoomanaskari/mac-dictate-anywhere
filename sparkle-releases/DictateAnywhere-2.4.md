# Dictate Anywhere 2.4

- Redesigned the app with a new visual identity, app icon, typography, navigation, settings, and dictation controls.
- Added Apple Speech as a transcription engine on supported Macs running macOS 26 or later.
- Apple Speech uses Apple’s on-device speech recognition, so audio and transcription stay on your Mac.
- Added language selection and guided setup for Apple Speech, while keeping FluidAudio available as the local alternative.
